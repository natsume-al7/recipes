Japanese Pork Bowl

Description

Ingredients

  • 200g - Cooked Rice
  • 120g - Thinly Sliced Pork
  • 1/4 - Whole Onion
  • 1 tsp - Vegetable Oil

    • Sauce

  • 1/2 tbsp - Sake
  • 1/4 tsp - Salt
  • 1 tsp - Sesame Oil
  • 1/2 tsp - Minced Garlic
  • to taste - Black Pepper

    • Toppings

  • as needed - Chopped Green Onions
  • as needed - White Sesame Seeds

Steps

  1. Thinly slice onions.
  2. Slice pork into bite sized pieces.
  3. Heat pan with oil with medium heat and add pork.
    Cook until color changes.
  4. Add onions to the pan and simmer until soft, then add the sauce.
  5. Add rice to a bowl, then add pork and onions on top.
  6. To top it off, add chopped green onions and white sesame seeds to your liking!

Enjoy!
